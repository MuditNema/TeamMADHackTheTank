Eligibility

  • To be eligible for our scholarship program, you must meet the following criteria:
  • Be currently enrolled in a college or university
  • Have a minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Be a citizen or permanent resident of the United States
  • Submit a complete application before the deadline

Application Process

  • To apply for our scholarship program, you must:
  • Complete the scholarship application form
  • Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended
  • Submit two letters of recommendation
  • Submit a personal statement or essay outlining your academic and career goals, achievements, and community involvement
  • Submit any additional materials required by the scholarship program

Selection Process

  • The scholarship committee will review all complete applications and select the most qualified candidates.
  • The scholarship committee's decision is final and cannot be appealed.

Award Disbursement

  • If you are selected to receive the scholarship, you will be notified by email or phone.
  • You will receive your award in the form of a check or direct deposit.
  • You must follow any additional instructions provided by the scholarship program, such as attending an award ceremony or submitting additional documentation.
  • You must use the scholarship funds for educational expenses only.
